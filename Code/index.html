welcome to exqusite cores

a space where you can create a die shot of your very own central processing unit (cpu) core. what is a cpu core? it's the cpu's processor; it receives instructions, and performs calculations or operations in response. it is typically made up of 4 parts: the memory, the core, the cache, and the queue. what does all that mean? couldn't tell ya! luckily, all you really need to know is that a cpu core is a computer chip and close-up photos of these chips outside of their housing (aka "die shots") are super cool. try different combinations of core parts below to make a unique die shot
